07 — Brand Voice
The brand IS
Precise
Every royalty, every fee, every split — enforced on-chain. No ambiguity, no intermediaries.
Explosive
Not decorative disruption. Real systemic change for creators who were burned by the old model.
Uncompromising
The contract is the law. No overrides, no admin keys on royalties. Your fire, forever.
Human
Behind every token is an artist. Behind every royalty is a livelihood. We build for people.
The brand is NOT
Corporate
No grey suits, no VC decks, no "leverage synergies."
Hype-driven
No floor prices, no "GM", no speculative pump culture.
Luxury-for-luxury
Premium because it works, not because it's expensive.
Abstract
No vague promises. Concrete functions, visible on-chain.
The old system took everything and gave back nothing.
Molotov puts the royalty in the contract — not in a promise.
Every sale. Every time. No exceptions.
